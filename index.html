🌾 Recommended Farmer Actions
ACTION 1 — IMMEDIATE (0–2 days)
🌾 Early Harvest
If paddy is 75%+ mature, harvest now. Yield drops 20–30% but crop is saved from total loss.
✓ Saves ~70% yield Cost: Labour only
ACTION 2 — SHORT TERM (2–7 days)
📅 Delay Plantation
If in early sowing phase, delay by 7–14 days. Wait for saltwater to recede before transplanting seedlings.
✓ Zero crop loss Cost: None
ACTION 3 — SEASONAL SWITCH
🧬 Salt-Resistant Varieties
Switch to BRL 103 or BRL 105 — tolerates up to 8 PSU. Ben Tre DARD recommends these for Ba Tri & Binh Dai coastal areas.
✓ Survives up to 8 PSU Seed cost: ~$40/ha
ACTION 4 — LONG TERM ADAPTATION
🦐 Rice–Shrimp Rotation
Rotate rice with brackish shrimp farming. Ben Tre has 2,000 ha under hi-tech shrimp model in Binh Dai, Ba Tri & Thanh Phu. Income up to 5× rice farming.
✓ 5× income potential Setup: ~$200/ha
